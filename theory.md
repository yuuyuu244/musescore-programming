# Music Theory

## what is Scale?

a scale in music is a group of notes

### 2 types of scales
1. major scales
1. minor scales

### 2 type of tone

1. whole tone ( whole steps, 全音 )
   e.g. C → D
2. semitone ( half steps 半音 )
   e.g. C → C#

### scales
